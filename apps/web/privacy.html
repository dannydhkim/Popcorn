Privacy Policy

Last updated:

ReelTok (“ReelTok”, “we”, “our”, or “us”) respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information when you use reeltok.io.

Information We Collect

  • Account information: If you create an account, we may collect a username, email address, and basic profile information.
  • User-generated content: Reactions, comments, lists, and other content you voluntarily submit.
  • Usage data: Basic analytics such as page views, interactions, and feature usage to improve the product.
  • Technical data: Device type, browser, and approximate location (e.g., country or region).

How We Use Information

  • Operate and improve ReelTok
  • Provide discovery, recommendation, and community features
  • Communicate with users about updates or support requests
  • Maintain safety, integrity, and moderation

Movie & TV Metadata

ReelTok uses third-party APIs to retrieve public movie and television metadata such as titles, posters, descriptions, cast, and genres. This data is used solely for discovery, labeling, and organizational purposes.

ReelTok does not host, stream, or distribute full movies, television episodes, or other copyrighted video content.

Cookies & Analytics

We may use cookies or similar technologies for basic functionality and analytics. These help us understand how the site is used and improve the experience.

Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We may share data only with service providers necessary to operate ReelTok (such as hosting, authentication, or analytics services), and only as required for functionality.

Data Retention

We retain information for as long as necessary to provide the service or comply with legal obligations. Users may request deletion of their account and associated data.

Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights to access, correct, or delete your personal information. To make a request, contact us at: privacy@reeltok.io

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updates will be posted on this page with a revised “Last updated” date.
