About

ReelTok is a social discovery layer for film and television. Users can browse titles, post reactions, follow creators, and build lists. To accurately label content and improve discovery, ReelTok uses a third-party metadata provider for title information such as posters, descriptions, cast, and genres.

How we use movie data

ReelTok uses metadata for:

  • Searching and matching titles (movie/TV)
  • Displaying posters and basic title details
  • Tagging posts and lists to the correct title
  • Linking to cast/crew and genre context

ReelTok does not provide full video, stream content, or host copyrighted media.
